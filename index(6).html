Our Domain

The Gated Shifter serves collectors of historically significant automobiles, including Ferrari, Lamborghini, Maserati, Bizzarrini, Bugatti, Alfa Romeo Zagato, and air-cooled Porsche, among other rare and consequential motorcars.

Our work is informed by more than five decades of direct mechanical, archival, and custodial experience—spanning the stewardship of some of the world's most significant private collections, concours-level judging, and leadership of the largest independent Ferrari workshop in the United States.
